FAQ

Questions Parents Often Ask

Is Bal Jyoti Public School co-educational?
Yes. It is a co-educational, English-medium day school affiliated to CBSE, serving Nursery through Class XII.
Is school transport available for my area?
Bus pick-up and drop-off is available across parts of Lal Kuan and Ghaziabad. Please check with the office to confirm your specific route.
What is the minimum age for Nursery admission?
Age criteria are set each academic year in line with CBSE and state norms. Please confirm the current cut-off age with the school office when you enquire.
Does the school offer a hostel or boarding facility?
No. Bal Jyoti is a day school; it does not currently offer hostel or boarding facilities.
How do I schedule a campus visit?
Message the school office on WhatsApp or call ahead, and the team will help you fix a convenient time to tour the campus.
What documents are required at the time of admission?
Generally the child's birth certificate, previous report card, transfer certificate (where applicable), address proof, Aadhaar copies and photographs. The office will confirm the exact list for your child's class.
