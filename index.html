About Us

A campus built on discipline, warmth and steady growth

Bal Jyoti Public School was established in 1987 and is managed by the Krishna Gopal Samitee, Uttar Pradesh. Located on GT Road at Lal Kuan, near NH-24 in Ghaziabad, the school has grown into a co-educational institution spread across a campus of roughly 8,855 square metres.

The school is affiliated to the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) and offers English-medium education from Nursery through Class XII. Over the years it has built a reputation locally as a school that keeps academics rigorous while making room for art, sport and community life.

Our approach stays close to the meaning of our name — Bal Jyoti, "the light of children." Teachers act as steady guides who help students reason, question and build confidence, rather than simply memorise for exams. Alongside academics, the school places real emphasis on discipline, ethics and respect as everyday habits, not once-a-year lessons.

Academics

A clear path from first steps to the final board exam

Every stage builds on the one before it — from a child's first day in Nursery to the choices they make in the Senior Secondary years.

Nursery – UKG

Pre-Primary

A gentle, activity-based introduction to school life — spoken language, motor skills, numbers and social habits, taught through play, stories and rhymes.

Class I – V

Primary

Core literacy and numeracy are built up alongside EVS, computer familiarity and the start of formal art, music and physical education periods.

Class VI – VIII

Middle School

Subjects widen into full Science, Social Science and Mathematics streams, with growing independence expected in homework, projects and classroom discussion.

Class IX – X

Secondary

Structured preparation for the CBSE Class X board examination, with regular assessments, doubt-clearing sessions and skill-based subjects as per the CBSE framework.

Class XI – XII

Senior Secondary

Stream-based study (Science / Commerce / Humanities, subject to availability) with focused board-exam preparation and guidance toward higher education and career choices.

Facilities

What the campus offers every day

School Transport

Bus pick-up and drop-off service is available for students commuting from across the Lal Kuan and Ghaziabad area.

Library

A dedicated reading space with reference books, textbooks and fiction to build a habit of reading beyond the classroom.

Science & Computer Labs

Practical, hands-on lab periods that turn Science and Computer Science lessons into real experiments and exercises.

Safety & Security

CCTV surveillance across the premises and supervised entry/exit help keep the day-to-day campus environment secure.

Sports & Play Areas

Open grounds and structured sports periods support the annual Sports Day along with regular physical education classes.

Well-Equipped Classrooms

Classrooms are designed to support both traditional teaching and interactive, activity-based lessons across grade levels.
