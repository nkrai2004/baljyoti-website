Who We Are

Inspiring Excellence Since 1986

Providing quality education with strong values, academic excellence, and holistic development.

For nearly four decades, Bal Jyoti Public School has been committed to providing quality education that nurtures confident learners, responsible citizens, and future leaders. Established in 1986, the school has earned the trust of thousands of families through its dedication to academic excellence, strong values, and holistic development.

Affiliated with the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE), Bal Jyoti Public School offers education from Nursery to Class XII in a safe, inclusive, and stimulating environment where every child is encouraged to discover their unique potential.

Our experienced team of 55+ dedicated educators believes every child learns differently. Through innovative teaching methods, personalized attention, and activity-based learning, we help students develop strong academic foundations along with creativity, communication, leadership, and life skills.

Education at Bal Jyoti Public School extends beyond textbooks. Students actively participate in sports, cultural activities, science exhibitions, technology-enabled learning, environmental awareness programmes, and community service initiatives that prepare them for future success.

With a modern campus designed for 2,000 students, smart classrooms, science laboratories, computer labs, library, sports facilities, and a caring atmosphere, we provide an environment where children enjoy learning every day.

Our partnership with parents forms the cornerstone of every student's success. Together, we strive to create a community where discipline, respect, compassion, integrity, and excellence become lifelong values.
