FAQ

Questions Candidates Often Ask

How do I apply for a teaching or staff position?
Email your resume to info@baljyoti.com mentioning the position and subject/grade you're applying for, or message the school office on WhatsApp to ask about current openings.
Do I need prior teaching experience?
Requirements vary by role and level. Some positions welcome freshers with the right qualification, while senior subject roles typically prefer prior classroom experience. Mention your experience level in your application either way.
Is B.Ed. mandatory for teaching roles?
For most CBSE teaching positions a B.Ed. or equivalent teaching qualification is required or preferred, in line with CBSE norms. Specific requirements are confirmed at the time of hiring for each role.
What is the salary range for teaching positions?
Compensation depends on the role, subject, grade level and experience, and is discussed directly during the interview process rather than published in advance.
Are part-time or substitute teaching roles available?
Occasionally, depending on the school's needs during a given session. Mention your availability and preference in your application and the office will let you know what's open.
How long does the hiring process usually take?
It varies with the role and the time of year, but candidates who are shortlisted are typically contacted for a screening call within a couple of weeks of applying.
